The Code

Server Rules

01
Roleplay Always

Stay in character at all times. This is a serious RP server — immersion is everything.

02
No RDM / VDM

Random and Vehicle Deathmatch are strictly forbidden. Every action needs an RP reason.

03
Value Your Life

Act as your character would in real danger. No reckless decisions during active RP scenarios.

04
No Meta-Gaming

Using out-of-character information in-game is prohibited. Keep IC and OOC completely separate.

05
Respect Everyone

Harassment, discrimination, or toxic behaviour results in an immediate permanent ban.

06
No Cheating / Exploiting

Zero tolerance for hacks, mods, or bug exploits. Report issues to staff immediately.
