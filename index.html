The Code

Server Rules

01
Roleplay Always
Stay in character at all times. This is a serious RP server — immersion is everything.
02
No RDM / VDM
Random Deathmatch and Vehicle Deathmatch are strictly forbidden. Every action needs RP reason.
03
Value Your Life
Act as your character would in real danger. Don't make reckless decisions during active RP scenarios.
04
No Meta-Gaming
Using out-of-character information in-game is prohibited. Keep IC and OOC completely separate.
05
Respect Everyone
Harassment, discrimination, or toxic behaviour will result in an immediate and permanent ban.
06
No Cheating / Exploiting
Zero tolerance for hacks, mods, or exploiting bugs. Report issues to staff immediately.
